Our Partnership

El Nada Hospital and O7 Therapy have come together to form a partnership that cares for the overall health of mothers.
El Nada Hospital, as the largest birthing center in Cairo and the leading maternity hospital in Egypt, is dedicated to the safety, wellbeing, and healthcare of women and children.

O7 Therapy provides accessible mental healthcare for people of all life stages and experiences, through the O7 mobile app. O7 has a selection of professionals specialized in women’s mental health and wellness, including one-on-one care for mothers throughout all perinatal phases, from pregnancy to post-birth, and parenting. Together, El Nada and O7 complete the circle of care of mothers and families.

Pregnancy, childbirth, and becoming a mom, brings tremendous change to a woman’s life. These changes influence your identity and sense of self, your relationship with your body and your emotions, and in your connection with others.

Stages of motherhood come in waves of different feelings and experiences; some of them joyous and fulfilling, while others waves, overwhelming. These waves can be challenging and conflicting to feel, and particularly difficult to share. Often the expectation that you have to be ready to be a mom, with all the skills and capacity required, can stop someone from seeking help when it is truly needed.
