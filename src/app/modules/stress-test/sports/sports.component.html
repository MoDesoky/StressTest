Meet Dr.

Sherif Zaher

Dr. Zaher has a doctorate in clinical psychology, a masters in Kinesiology (sports performance) and a bachelor's in psychology. He’s also a licensed clinical psychologist in the state of CA., and continues to offer his clinical and sport psychology services to clients via telehealth. Dr. Zaher’s clinical background includes but is not limited to the following positions: junior specialist with UC Davis Children's Hospital, mental health rehabilitation specialist at a crisis residential center, and a psychological assistant at an outpatient mental health clinic. He considers his involvement in a patient's recovery process a great honor, and focuses on adopting an eclectic approach that would provide each client with the necessary treatment that would help in overcoming the presenting symptoms experienced. He has a special interest in assisting patients struggling with depression and anxiety related disorders. Dr. Zaher’s area of expertise is sport psychology, where he has been working with high level athletes for 20+ years.

This Elite Training Program

will prepare you to help your clients:

Develop mental plans for training and competition.

Understand the factors associated with peak performance.

Self-regulate and successfully perform in stressful or anxiety provoking situations.

Apply the science behind performing “in the zone”.

Increase self-awareness of triggers and barriers resulting in sub-par performance.

Enhance team cohesion and creating a positive mastery focused team environment.

Improve skills associated with positive performance: confidence, motivation, resilience, self-talk, and concentration.

Develop various mental tools for performance enhancement (ex. imagery, goal setting, mindfulness).

Successfully transition from sport or any other career.

Improve recovery and rehabilitation from injury.

Program Certification

Successfully completing the program offers a certification which demonstrates to clients, and the public at large that you have met the highest standards of professional practice, including:

Completing a combination of educational and clinical requirements.

Successfully passing a certification exam.

Receiving clinical supervision by a licensed psychologist (Dr. Sherif Zaher).

Adhering to ethical principles and committing to ongoing professional development.

*Please note that gaining certification doesn’t guarantee employment opportunities. However, O7 certified candidates will be used to fill the demand of clients as needed.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Masters degree in either sport science or psychology (or equivalent/closely related field)
  • Demonstrated expertise in working with clients to optimize performance, enjoyment, and/or personal development in sport or other domains
  • Minimum 2 years of clinical experience 
  • Fluent in both English and Arabic
